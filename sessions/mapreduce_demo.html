Independent verification

Every Reduce total below is checked against a second calculation, done a completely different way (a straight filter-and-sum over the raw 24 rows, no Map/Shuffle involved). If they match, the pipeline computed correctly — it says nothing about whether the original 24 receipts were entered correctly.

⚠️

What this check does NOT prove: if a receipt had the wrong city, a typo'd amount, or a duplicate entry, both calculations would agree on the same wrong number. This stamp verifies the engine, not the ledger.

Input

Relabel each record by the key you care about. No math yet.

Shuffle

Physically regroup so same-key records sit together.

Reduce

Aggregate each group into one final business number.
